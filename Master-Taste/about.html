Master Taste: A Melbourne Culinary Journey

Check out News Say Hi

In the vibrant heart of Melbourne, in 2015, the dream of "Master Taste" was born. Its founder, Ling Wu, a Beijing native, had always been passionate about bringing the authentic flavors of her hometown to the food enthusiasts of Australia.Ling had grown up in a family where meals were more than just food; they were a tapestry of stories, traditions, and ancestral wisdom. With memories of her grandmother's dumplings, the spicy thrill of Sichuan noodles, and the comforting embrace of Cantonese rice dishes, she was determined to share these treasures with a new audience.

With the support of her family and a dedicated team, Master Taste opened its doors in late 2016. They kept their menu focused, Master Taste opened its doors in late 2016. Each dish was a testament to traditional methods, with a slight Melbourne twist, ensuring both authenticity and a touch of local flavor. Word quickly spread about the restaurant that served not just food, but memories. The dumplings, reminiscent of northern China's cold winters, the noodles spicy enough to challenge the bravest, and rice dishes that comforted the soul.

By 2018, Master Taste had become a must-visit spot in Melbourne, with locals and tourists alike queuing up to experience Ling's culinary magic. The restaurant also started hosting monthly cultural evenings, introducing Melburnians to Chinese traditions, festivals, and, of course, food. Today, Master Taste stands as a testament to Ling's vision, determination, and love for her culinary heritage. As she often says, "Every dish has a story, and we're here to share it with Melbourne."
